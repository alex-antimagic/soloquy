Data Sources

Financial metrics can come from:

  • QuickBooks: Automatically synced from P&L reports
  • Manual Entry: Entered by HR administrators
  • CRM: Calculated from won deals
Data Integrity

Best practices:

  • Finalize metrics before calculating bonuses
  • QuickBooks sync is most accurate
  • Manual entries can be updated anytime
  • Bonuses only calculate once per month
Monthly Workflow
  1. Enter or sync financial data
  2. Review bonus rules
  3. Calculate bonuses
  4. Approve in Compensation
