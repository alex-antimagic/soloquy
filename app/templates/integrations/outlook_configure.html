Azure AD Setup Instructions
1. Sign in to Azure Portal

Go to Azure Portal and sign in with your Microsoft account

2. Register App

Navigate to "Azure Active Directory" → "App registrations" → "New registration"

  • Name: "{{ g.current_tenant.name }} - Soloquy"
  • Supported account types: "Accounts in this organizational directory only"
3. Configure Authentication

In your app's "Authentication" section:

  • Add platform: "Web"
  • Add redirect URIs:
    {{ url_for('integrations.outlook_callback', scope='workspace', _external=True) }}
    {{ url_for('integrations.outlook_callback', scope='user', _external=True) }}
  • Enable "Access tokens" and "ID tokens"
4. Create Client Secret

In "Certificates & secrets" → "Client secrets" → "New client secret"

  • Description: "Soloquy"
  • Expiration: Your choice (24 months recommended)
  • Copy the secret value immediately (you can't see it again)
5. API Permissions

In "API permissions" → "Add a permission" → "Microsoft Graph" → "Delegated permissions":

  • Mail.ReadWrite
  • Mail.Send
  • Calendars.ReadWrite
  • offline_access

Optional: Click "Grant admin consent" to pre-approve for all workspace users.

Note: This same Azure AD app will be used for all users in your workspace. Each user authenticates with their own credentials and gets their own access token.

How This Works
OAuth Authentication

When you click "Continue to Microsoft Sign-In", you'll:

  1. Sign in with your Microsoft credentials
  2. Grant permission for Soloquy to access your mailbox
  3. Get redirected back here with your personal access token
Privacy: Your access token only grants access to your mailbox. No one else in the workspace can access your emails.
After Connecting

You'll be able to enable Outlook access for your personal AI agents. They can:

  • Read and search your emails
  • Send emails on your behalf
  • Access your calendar
  • Manage shared mailboxes you have access to
Token Expiry: Your access token is automatically refreshed. You may need to re-authenticate if it expires or is revoked.
