How to Get QuickBooks OAuth Credentials
  1. Go to QuickBooks Developer Portal
  2. Create a new app or select an existing one
  3. Navigate to "Keys & OAuth" section
  4. Copy your Client ID and Client Secret
  5. Add this redirect URI to your QuickBooks app: {{ url_for('integrations.quickbooks_callback', _external=True) }}
  6. Select the scopes: Accounting
  7. Paste your credentials above and click "Save Credentials"
