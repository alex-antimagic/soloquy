Understanding Integration Access

Soloquy uses a secure integration model to protect your data:

  • Workspace Integrations (Admin-configured) - Shared accounts like support@company.com accessible to all workspace agents
  • Personal Integrations (Your accounts) - Your personal Gmail, Outlook, or Drive accessible ONLY to agents YOU create

Security: Pre-set workspace agents cannot access your personal integrations. To use your personal accounts, create your own agent.
